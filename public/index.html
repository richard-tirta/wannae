    About Us

    Wanna-E food truck is founded by four food enthusiasts from Mandalay, Myanmar. Our passion is to deliver authentic hometown Mandalay foods to all food lovers in California.

    Mandalay, located at the center of Myanmar, is well known for its diverse culture and foods. Several decades ago immigrants from the Yunnan province of China came to Mandalay and have since helped influence and transform the cuisine that we love so much. This influence along with more with more subtle contributions from near by cultures such as India and Thailand come through in our style of Authentic Mandalay Food that we offer you.

    From our hometown to lovely California we sincerely welcome you to our food truck and find great pleasure in serving you the food of our homeland.
